Tutorials

  1. How to use the "Rochester Cloak" for a Science Lab.

    The 2015 AAPT BFY 2 (College Park, MD) presentation (citation information in Presentation section) can be used for a lab course, ranging from beginning level to advanced level, depending on how much students are asked to do and how good of a background image they should obtain. The presentation includes:

    1. Invisibility cloaking background
    2. Tutorial: Geometric optics, ABCD matrix
    3. Problem sets for students: ABCD matrix and paraxial cloaking
    4. Paraxial cloaking theory and experiments
    5. Possible research ideas
    6. Lab suggestions, demonstration guidelines
    7. Specifications for lenses to order and thick lens calculations.

    Presentation Download: Low-resolution version. High resolution version.
